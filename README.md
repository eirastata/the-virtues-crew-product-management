# The Virtues Crew — Product Owner Case Study

## Overview

This repository presents a Product Owner case study for The Virtues Crew, a children's educational mobile app designed to help kids learn values through stories, music, and prayers.

The purpose of this project is to demonstrate Product Owner practices using Agile and Scrum concepts.

---

## My Role

Product Owner

Responsibilities included:

• Defining the product vision  
• Creating and maintaining the product roadmap  
• Managing and prioritizing the product backlog  
• Writing user stories following INVEST principles  
• Defining product success metrics  

---

## Product Vision

The goal of The Virtues Crew is to provide a safe, engaging, and meaningful digital experience for children while giving parents confidence in the content their children consume.

---
