# Product Vision — The Virtues Crew

## Vision Statement

The Virtues Crew aims to provide a safe and engaging mobile experience where children can learn values through stories, music, and prayers.

## Target Users

Primary users:

Children aged 3–10

Secondary users:

Parents seeking safe and educational content

## Problem

Parents struggle to find safe and meaningful digital content for children.

## Solution

A mobile app offering curated stories, prayers, and music designed specifically for kids.

## Product Goal

Increase engagement, retention, and user satisfaction.
